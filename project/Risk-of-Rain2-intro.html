Game Characteristics
  • The original name:Risk of Rain 2
  • Developer: Hopoo Games
  • Distribution company: Gearbox Publishing
  • Game type: Roguelike
  • Release date: March 29, 2019
  • Game platform: Steam,NS,PS4
  • Number of players: single and multiple
  • Game screen: 3D

Introducing "Assassin's Creed Valhalla" The classic dungeon-exploration multiplayer game "Risk of Rain" is presented in 3D and can be played as a single player or in a team of up to four friends, all the way through a series of battles against a host of monsters, unlocking new loot, and ultimately finding a way to escape the planet. As players venture through Risk of Rain 2, there are many altars on the map that offer a variety of buff bonuses. The game will provide a large number of random props, players can build their own characters according to their own choice, random can bring better experience.

Game propaganda They fought their way out of the clutches of a monstrous monster and escaped disaster on a chaotic alien planet. Fight alone, or fight with your friends. Combine trophies in unexpected ways and master each character until you become the terror threat you feared when you made your first crash landing
